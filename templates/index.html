Frequently Asked Questions

What should a bank reconciliation template include?

A bank reconciliation template needs: transaction date, description, reference number, bank amount, book amount, cleared status, and variance. The summary section should show ending bank balance, outstanding deposits, outstanding checks, and adjusted book balance.

What chart of accounts categories do most small businesses need?

Core categories: Assets (checking, savings, accounts receivable, fixed assets), Liabilities (accounts payable, credit cards, loans), Equity (owner's equity, retained earnings), Revenue (sales, service income), Expenses (cost of goods, payroll, rent, utilities, professional fees).

How do I create a month-end close checklist for a bookkeeping firm?

Standard steps: collect bank statements, convert and import transactions, reconcile each account, review uncategorized items, check AR/AP aging, run trial balance, deliver financial statements to client. A checklist template for this is available at zerabooks.com/templates.
