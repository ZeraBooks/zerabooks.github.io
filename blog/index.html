Frequently Asked Questions

How do accountants automate bank statement processing?

AI-powered tools like Zera Books extract transactions from PDF bank statements, auto-categorize them against a chart of accounts, and export directly to QuickBooks or Xero — eliminating manual data entry.

What is the fastest way to convert a PDF bank statement to Excel?

Upload the PDF to an AI converter. The tool extracts all transactions, normalizes date and description formatting, and downloads as a formatted Excel file — typically under 60 seconds per statement.

Can AI categorize bank transactions automatically?

Yes. AI tools trained on bookkeeping data can match transactions to QuickBooks or Xero chart of accounts categories with 95%+ accuracy. Zera Books assigns a confidence score to each categorization.
