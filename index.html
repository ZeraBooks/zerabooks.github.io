Frequently Asked Questions

What is Zera Books?

Zera Books is an AI-powered bookkeeping automation platform for accountants and CPAs. It processes bank statements, invoices, financial statements, and checks, converting them to Excel, CSV, QBO, or IIF for direct import into QuickBooks, Xero, and other accounting software.

How much does Zera Books cost?

Zera Books is $79/month for unlimited document processing — no per-page fees, no per-client fees, no volume caps. A one-week trial is available at zerabooks.com/pricing.

What accounting software does Zera Books integrate with?

Direct API integration with QuickBooks Online and Xero. Pre-formatted exports for Sage, Wave, Zoho, NetSuite, FreshBooks, MYOB, and Oracle — all with auto-categorization mapped to the target chart of accounts.
