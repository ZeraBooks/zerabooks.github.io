Month-End Close Automation

Zera Books month-end close automation handles the document-processing bottleneck that slows down closing cycles. When statements arrive late, manual data entry backs up, and reconciliation can't start until the data is in the system. Zera Books compresses that bottleneck.

The slowest part of month-end isn't reviewing numbers — it's getting the data in. Zera Books automates the ingestion, extraction, categorization, and import steps.

Where Month-End Gets Delayed

A typical month-end close for a bookkeeping firm involves: waiting for client statements, manually entering or copying transactions, categorizing each one against a chart of accounts, reconciling against the accounting system balance, and resolving discrepancies. The manual steps — entry and categorization — are where time goes.

Zera Books eliminates the manual entry step entirely and handles categorization automatically for the majority of transactions.

Batch Processing for Multi-Client Close

For firms closing multiple clients simultaneously, the bottleneck multiplies. Batch processing lets you upload all client statements at once — 50+ files in a single upload — and process them in parallel. While one month's statements are processing, you're reviewing the previous batch. The close cycle compresses significantly.

Handling Late or Incomplete Statements

Clients often send partial data — January through November, then December arrives a week later. Zera Books handles incremental uploads. Process what you have, then add the missing month. Duplicate detection ensures that if there's any overlap between statement periods, transactions don't get double-counted in the final output.
