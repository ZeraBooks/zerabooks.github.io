Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert a bank statement PDF to QBO format?

Upload the PDF to Zera Books, select QBO as the output format, and download the file. In QuickBooks Online, go to Banking > File Upload and import the QBO file directly — no column mapping required.

How do I import a CSV bank statement into QuickBooks Online?

In QuickBooks Online, go to Banking > Upload from file. Upload your CSV, then map the date, description, and amount columns. QuickBooks will categorize transactions using its existing rules.

How long does it take to convert a 12-month bank statement to Excel?

With an AI converter, a 12-month multi-page PDF typically converts in 30–90 seconds depending on page count and whether the document is scanned or digital.
