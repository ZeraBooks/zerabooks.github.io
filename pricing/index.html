Honest Edge Cases — When Per-Page Might Make Sense

Zera Books is designed for accounting practices, not individuals doing occasional personal finance work. Here are the scenarios where cheaper alternatives might be appropriate:

Fewer than 5 Clients

At very low volume, per-page costs may be lower. But factor in 30+ minutes of manual categorization per client — at 5 clients that's 2.5 hours/month. Your time has value.

One-Time Conversions

Need to convert a single statement once and never again? A basic tool or even a free online converter may suffice. Zera Books is built for recurring monthly workflows, not one-off jobs.
