When Per-Page Pricing Costs More

A bookkeeping firm handling 10 clients, each with 3 months of statements averaging 15 pages each, is processing 450 pages per conversion cycle. At $0.10–$0.50/page (common ranges for per-page tools), that's $45–$225 just for one batch. At $79/month flat, you're covered regardless.

Firms that process at volume — monthly statement pulls, batch reconciliations, year-end cleanup — see the biggest savings with flat-rate pricing.
