Client Management Dashboard — Zera Books

The Zera Books client management dashboard is built for accounting firms and bookkeeping teams that handle documents across multiple clients. It organizes conversions by client, keeps a full history of every processed file, and makes it possible to manage 20 or 200 clients from one screen without extra per-client fees.

One flat price covers all clients. No per-client fees, no per-user licenses. The $79/month plan supports unlimited clients.

What the Client Dashboard Does

  • Client folders: Create a folder per client. Upload and convert documents directly into the client's workspace.
  • Conversion history: Every conversion is logged — source file name, document type, output format, date processed, and download links.
  • Multi-client workflow: Process documents for multiple clients in parallel. No need to log in and out or switch accounts.
  • Audit trail: Each conversion record is timestamped and retained. Useful for client billing, QA review, and year-end documentation.
  • Version control: If you process an updated statement for a client, the previous version is retained in history rather than overwritten.

How It Fits Into a Firm Workflow

A typical CPA firm workflow at month-end: clients send PDFs by email or through a client portal. Statements land in the Zera Books dashboard, assigned to the right client folder. One person processes 15 clients' statements in a batch. Outputs land in each client's folder, ready to push to QuickBooks or export to Excel.

Without a client management layer, this workflow requires manual file renaming, separate downloads per client, and a separate tracking system to know what's been processed. The Zera Books dashboard handles all of that natively.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Zera Books handle multiple clients?

Zera Books has a client management dashboard where you create client folders, assign conversions to clients, and track conversion history per client. All clients are managed from one account at no extra per-client fee.

Is there a per-client fee in Zera Books?

No. Zera Books charges a flat $79/month regardless of how many clients you manage. There are no per-client or per-user fees.

Can I see the conversion history for a specific client in Zera Books?

Yes. The Zera Books client dashboard shows full conversion history per client, including source files, output files, timestamps, and document types processed.
