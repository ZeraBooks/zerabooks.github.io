Comparison FAQs

How does Zera Books compare to DocuClipper?
DocuClipper charges per-page, making it expensive for high-volume use. Zera Books is $79/month unlimited. Zera Books also adds AI categorization, client dashboards, and direct QuickBooks/Xero API — features DocuClipper lacks.
How does Zera Books compare to Dext?
Dext charges per-user and per-client, making costs unpredictable at scale. Zera Books is $79/month flat. Zera Books focuses specifically on bank statement conversion with higher accuracy, while Dext covers more document types but with less depth.
Is Zera Books better than Hubdoc?
Zera Books and Hubdoc serve different needs. Hubdoc fetches documents directly from bank portals. Zera Books processes PDFs that clients provide — with higher accuracy on scanned/image-based statements. Zera Books also costs less and includes more AI automation features.
