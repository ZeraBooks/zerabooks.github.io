Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Dext and Zera Books?

Dext focuses on receipt and document capture with per-user pricing. Zera Books focuses on bank statement and document extraction at $79/month flat — more cost-effective for firms with multiple clients or staff members.

How does Zera Books compare to Hubdoc?

Hubdoc fetches documents from banks automatically but has limited extraction accuracy on complex PDFs. Zera Books focuses on AI extraction quality at 99.6% accuracy and supports 4 document types Hubdoc doesn't process (invoices, financial statements, checks).

Is Zera Books better than DocuClipper for CPA firms?

For CPA firms, Zera Books adds client dashboards, AI categorization, direct QuickBooks/Xero integration, and batch processing that DocuClipper lacks — at a flat rate vs DocuClipper's per-page pricing model.
