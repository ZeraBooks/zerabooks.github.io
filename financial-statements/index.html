Financial Statement Processing — Zera Books

Zera Books financial statement processing converts PDF profit and loss statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements into structured Excel or CSV files. It preserves account hierarchy, captures subtotals, and handles multi-period comparative formats.

Why this matters: Most bank statement converters don't handle financial statements at all. Zera Books is one of the few tools that processes all four document types — bank statements, financial statements, invoices, and checks — under one platform.

Supported Financial Statement Types

Statement TypeWhat's Extracted
Profit & Loss (Income Statement)Revenue lines, expense lines, COGS, gross profit, net income, subtotals per section
Balance SheetAsset accounts, liability accounts, equity accounts, section subtotals, total assets/liabilities
Cash Flow StatementOperating, investing, and financing activity lines, net cash change, opening/closing balance
Multi-period comparativeAll of the above with period columns preserved side-by-side

Output Structure

The Excel output uses one row per line item. Columns include: account name, account code (when present), section/category, and one amount column per period. This structure is ready for pivot tables or direct import into financial analysis tools.

For multi-period statements — for example a comparative P&L showing Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4 — each period becomes a separate column rather than requiring manual column-by-column extraction.

Account Hierarchy Preservation

Financial statements have nested structures: Revenue → Operating Revenue → Product Sales, for instance. Zera Books captures the hierarchy level for each line item so the output retains the logical grouping from the original document. Section headers and subtotal rows are flagged separately from data rows.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of financial statements does Zera Books process?

Zera Books processes profit and loss statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements. It handles single-period and multi-period formats, extracting line items, account codes, subtotals, and period headers.

Can Zera Books convert a PDF P&L statement to Excel?

Yes. Zera Books converts PDF P&L statements to Excel or CSV, preserving account hierarchy, subtotals, and period columns. No template configuration is needed.

Does Zera Books support multi-period financial statement extraction?

Yes. Zera Books handles comparative financial statements with multiple period columns and outputs each period as a separate column in the Excel or CSV file.
