AP Automation: What Firms Actually Need

Enterprise AP platforms like Tipalti and Coupa are built for companies processing their own payables at scale. For accounting firms managing AP for clients, the workflow is different: receive invoice PDFs from clients, extract data, code to the correct expense account, and post to the client's QuickBooks or Xero file.

The bottleneck in this workflow is data extraction and coding — not payment processing. The right tool for a bookkeeping firm is one that extracts invoice data accurately and connects directly to the client's accounting software.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is accounts payable automation?

AP automation uses software to capture invoice data from PDFs, extract line items and payment details, route for approval, and post to accounting software — replacing manual data entry in the payables workflow.

What is the best AP automation software for small accounting firms?

For small accounting firms handling AP for multiple clients, Zera Books is the most cost-effective — invoice OCR, line item extraction, and QuickBooks/Xero integration at $79/month flat with no per-client or per-invoice fees.

Pricing varies widely: enterprise platforms like Tipalti run $500+/month, mid-market tools like Bill.com run $45–$79/user/month, and accounting-focused AI tools like Zera Books are $79/month flat for unlimited volume.
