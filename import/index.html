Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import a QBO file into QuickBooks Online?

In QuickBooks Online, go to Banking > Upload from file, select your QBO file, then choose the bank account to link it to. Transactions appear in the For Review tab for confirmation.

How do I import bank transactions into Xero?

In Xero, go to Accounting > Bank Accounts, select the account, click Import a statement, and upload an OFX or CSV file. Xero parses the transactions and adds them to the reconciliation queue.

What causes a QuickBooks import error with a CSV file?

Common causes: date format mismatch, amount columns with currency symbols, unrecognized header names, or extra empty rows. Use Zera Books to export a pre-formatted QBO file to avoid these issues entirely.
