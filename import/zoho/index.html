Zoho Books CSV Import Requirements

Zoho Books Banking module accepts CSV files with flexible column options. Unlike most platforms, Zoho supports both a single signed Amount column and separate Debit/Credit columns.

ColumnRequiredFormatNotes
DateRequiredMatches org localeMM/DD/YYYY for US, DD/MM/YYYY for non-US. Set in Zoho organization settings.
DescriptionRequiredTextTransaction memo. Used for auto-categorization rule matching.
AmountRequired*Signed decimalNegative for debits, positive for credits. Or use separate Debit/Credit columns.
DebitOptional*Positive decimalWithdrawal amount. Use with Credit instead of signed Amount.
CreditOptional*Positive decimalDeposit amount. Use with Debit instead of signed Amount.
ReferenceOptionalTextCheck number or bank transaction reference.

Either Amount or Debit/Credit required — not both. Zera Books Zoho export uses a single signed Amount column with the date format matching your Zoho organization locale. Select US or International when exporting.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import bank transactions into Zoho Books?

Banking (left sidebar) > select account > Import Statement. Upload your CSV, map columns, and import. Transactions appear for categorization and reconciliation.

What CSV format does Zoho Books require?

Date (locale-appropriate), Description, and either signed Amount or separate Debit/Credit columns. Zera Books Zoho export handles locale and format automatically.

Can I import a PDF bank statement into Zoho Books?

No. Convert to CSV first. Zera Books converts any PDF — digital or scanned — to Zoho-ready CSV in under 2 minutes with 99.6% accuracy.

Where is the bank import option in Zoho Books?

Banking module in the left sidebar > select your bank account > Import Statement. It's in Banking, not the main Accounting module.
