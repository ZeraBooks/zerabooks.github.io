What Changes by Industry

The chart of accounts categories that transactions get coded to differ by industry. A restaurant client needs Food Cost, Labor Cost, and Occupancy as primary expense categories. A law firm needs separate Trust Account and Operating Account ledgers. Zera Books' AI recognizes transaction descriptions and adapts categorization accordingly.

Transaction volume also differs significantly — a busy restaurant may generate 800+ transactions per month across accounts, where a small law firm might have 60. Batch processing and multi-account handling become more important at the high-volume end.
