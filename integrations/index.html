How the QuickBooks Online API Integration Works

Most tools require downloading a QBO file and manually uploading it to QuickBooks. Zera Books eliminates those steps with a true API push. Here's the exact workflow.

1

Connect Once with OAuth 2.0

Click "Connect to QuickBooks" in your Zera Books dashboard. Authorize via QuickBooks OAuth — a 30-second setup. Connection is saved for all future conversions.

2

Convert the Statement

Upload your PDF bank statement. Zera AI extracts transactions with 99.6% accuracy, assigns categories from your QBO chart of accounts, and prepares the structured payload.

3

Review and Approve (Optional)

Review extracted transactions and category suggestions before pushing. Modify any categories. Bulk-approve all transactions with one click if everything looks correct.

4

Push Directly to QuickBooks

Click "Push to QuickBooks." Transactions appear in your QBO bank feed within seconds, categorized and ready for reconciliation. No file download, no manual upload.

AI Categorization: How It Works Across Integrations

Every Zera Books integration includes AI transaction categorization. The AI learns your chart of accounts and applies consistent category suggestions — whether you're pushing to QuickBooks API or downloading a Sage CSV.

Training

Learns Your Chart of Accounts

Import your chart of accounts from QuickBooks, Xero, or manually. Zera AI maps transaction descriptions to your specific account codes — not generic categories.

Pattern Recognition

Vendor Pattern Memory

Once a vendor like "OFFICE DEPOT #1234" is categorized as "Office Supplies," every future transaction from that vendor auto-assigns the same category with 98%+ confidence.

Confidence Scores

Confidence-Based Review

High-confidence categorizations (95%+) are auto-applied. Low-confidence transactions are flagged for manual review — you only touch the ones that actually need attention.

Multi-Client

Per-Client Chart Learning

Each client in your dashboard has their own learned chart of accounts. The AI doesn't mix up categories between clients — even if two clients use the same bank.

Integration FAQs

Does Zera Books connect directly to QuickBooks Online?
Yes. Zera Books has a direct API integration with QuickBooks Online. Converted transactions are pushed directly to your QBO account without downloading or re-uploading any files.
How does Zera Books integrate with Xero?
Zera Books connects to Xero via direct API. Transactions are formatted to Xero's bank feed specifications and pushed directly to the correct bank account in Xero. No manual CSV upload needed.
Which accounting software does Zera Books support?
Direct API: QuickBooks Online and Xero. Pre-formatted exports: Sage 50/100, Wave, Zoho Books, FreshBooks, NetSuite, MYOB, and Oracle Financials.
Does Zera Books map transactions to my chart of accounts?
Yes. Zera AI learns your chart of accounts and suggests categories for every transaction. For QuickBooks and Xero, these are applied during the API push. For other software, category columns are included in the export.
