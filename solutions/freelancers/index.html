The Freelancer Bookkeeping Problem

Freelancers have the same bookkeeping obligations as any business — track income and expenses, categorize for tax purposes, reconcile against invoices. But unlike businesses with bookkeepers, freelancers do it themselves.

The typical freelancer approach: save statements throughout the year, then panic at tax time trying to sort through 12 months of PDFs. Zera Books for freelancers makes this a 30-minute monthly task instead of a two-day annual scramble.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can freelancers process their own bank statements with Zera Books?

Yes. Zera Books is designed for self-service. Upload your PDF and AI handles extraction and categorization. No accounting background required.

Does Zera Books work for both business and personal bank accounts?

Zera Books processes any bank statement. For freelancers with separate business and personal accounts, each account's statements can be processed separately with their own conversion history.

How does Zera Books handle mixed business and personal transactions?

AI categorizes every transaction. Transactions that are clearly personal (grocery stores, personal subscriptions) are flagged so you can mark them as non-business. You control the final categorization.

Can I give my accountant access to the processed data?

Yes. Export clean Excel or CSV files and share directly with your accountant. The export includes original amounts, dates, descriptions, and AI-assigned categories.
