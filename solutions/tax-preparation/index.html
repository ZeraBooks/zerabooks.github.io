The Tax Season Data Processing Problem

Tax accountants typically handle 50–200 client returns. Each client may send bank statements from multiple accounts spanning 12 months. The math adds up fast: 100 clients × 3 accounts × 12 months = 3,600 statement PDFs to process.

The real bottleneck isn't the tax analysis — it's getting clean, categorized data before you can start. Zera Books tax preparation workflow eliminates this bottleneck.

Batch upload by client

Upload an entire year's statements for a client at once. Zera Books processes all 12 months simultaneously and organizes outputs chronologically.

Auto-categorization for tax categories

AI categorizes transactions against QuickBooks or Xero chart of accounts — identifying business expenses, income, and deductible items automatically.

Year-over-year comparison

Conversion history retained indefinitely. Pull up last year's processed statements to compare income trends and flag unusual transactions quickly.

Audit-ready outputs

Every conversion logged with original source file. If a client gets audited, you can pull the source statement and processed output instantly.
