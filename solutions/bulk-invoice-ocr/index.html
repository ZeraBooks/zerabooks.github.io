Invoice Formats Supported

Invoice formats vary enormously — from structured PDFs generated by accounting software to scanned paper invoices and image files photographed on phones. Zera OCR handles them all.

Digital PDF invoices

QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks, and other accounting software generate these. Highest accuracy — text layer extracted directly.

Scanned invoices

Paper invoices run through a scanner. Zera OCR handles varying scan quality, rotation, and resolution without degrading accuracy.

Phone photos of invoices

JPG and PNG files from phones accepted. AI corrects perspective distortion and adjusts for lighting variations common in phone photos.

Multi-page invoice packets

Multi-page PDFs containing multiple invoices processed and separated automatically. Each invoice identified and extracted independently.

Frequently Asked Questions

What invoice formats does Zera Books support?

All formats — digital PDFs from accounting software, scanned paper invoices, JPG/PNG image files, and multi-page invoice packets. Zera OCR is purpose-built for financial documents at 99.6% accuracy.

Does Zera Books extract individual line items from invoices?

Yes. Line item extraction includes description, quantity, unit price, and subtotal per line. This is critical for accounts payable and cost allocation workflows.

How does Zera Books handle invoice tax calculations?

Tax rates, tax amounts, and tax types (GST, HST, VAT, sales tax) are extracted and validated against the line item subtotals. Discrepancies are flagged for review.

Can I process invoices in batch for multiple clients?

Yes. Each client has a dedicated folder. Upload invoices for all clients in one session — they route to the correct client workspace automatically.
