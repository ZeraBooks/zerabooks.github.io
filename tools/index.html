Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to manually enter bank statement transactions?

Manual bank statement entry averages 2–4 minutes per page for an experienced bookkeeper. A 12-page monthly statement takes roughly 24–48 minutes per client — around 5–10 hours per month for a firm with 10 active clients.

How do I calculate hours saved by automating bank statement processing?

Multiply average pages per statement by 2.5 minutes by number of clients by 12 months. Subtract 30 seconds per statement for AI processing time. For 20 clients averaging 8 pages, automation saves roughly 77 hours annually.

What is a good auto-match rate for bank reconciliation?

90%+ is considered good. Zera Books achieves 95%+ on standard datasets, meaning fewer than 5 in 100 transactions require manual review.
