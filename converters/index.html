Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between QBO, QIF, and OFX formats?

QBO is a proprietary Intuit format for direct bank feed import into QuickBooks. QIF is an older Intuit format still supported by QuickBooks Desktop. OFX is an open standard supported by QuickBooks, Xero, and most other accounting platforms.

Can I convert a PDF invoice to CSV?

Yes. Zera Books extracts line items, dates, vendor names, and totals from PDF invoices and exports to CSV or Excel, preserving all structured data from the original document.
