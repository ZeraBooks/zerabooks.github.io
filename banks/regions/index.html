Regions Bank Statement Formats and What Gets Extracted

Regions Bank primarily serves the southeastern and midwestern United States, and its statement formatting reflects a mix of legacy AmSouth Bancorporation and Union Planters layouts that were absorbed through mergers. Current Regions digital statements use a clean single-table format, but business account statements — particularly from Alabama, Tennessee, and Mississippi branches — still use a two-section format with checks listed separately from electronic transactions.

Zera Books dynamically processes all Regions statement formats. The AI recognizes the distinct Regions header block, identifies account sections, and extracts date, description, debit, credit, and balance columns into clean structured output without any configuration on your end.
