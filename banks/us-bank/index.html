US Bank Statement Formats and What Gets Extracted

US Bank uses a compact two-column transaction format for personal statements, with debits and credits listed inline rather than in separate columns. Business statements add check number columns and ACH trace references. US Bank Altitude and FlexPerks credit card statements use a distinct layout with reward point columns that need to be parsed separately from transaction amounts.

US Bank's digital PDFs from online banking are text-layer files that convert cleanly. Older statements from before 2018 and any that have been printed and scanned require OCR processing. Zera Books handles both paths automatically — no configuration required.
