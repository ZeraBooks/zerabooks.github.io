Huntington PDF Statement Format

Huntington personal account statements list transactions in a straightforward table: Date, Description, Credits (+), Debits (-), Balance. The 24-Hour Grace section at the bottom of the page is informational and does not contain separate transactions — it shows the grace period status for the period.

Business Checking PDFs use a grouped layout: Deposits, Checks, Electronic Credits, Electronic Debits, Other Debits. Each section ends with a subtotal row. When converting business statements, these subtotals must be excluded from the transaction list.

Huntington TCF Bank Accounts

Huntington acquired TCF Bank in 2021. Former TCF account holders may have statements that look different from standard Huntington statements, particularly for the transition period (2021–2022). These TCF-era statements use the older TCF layout but are otherwise standard PDFs. Zera Books handles both formats.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download Huntington Bank statements as PDF?

Log into Huntington Online Banking, select your account, then go to Statements. PDF statements are available for up to 7 years. You can also export transaction activity as CSV or OFX from the Account Activity screen.

Does Huntington Bank work with QuickBooks bank feeds?

Yes. Huntington supports direct QuickBooks Online bank feed connections. For older statements or accounts without feed access, download the PDF and convert to QBO with Zera Books.

What is unique about Huntington Bank PDF statements?

Huntington business PDFs sometimes include a 24-Hour Grace deposit notification section alongside the transaction table. Basic converters may extract the grace period balance figures as transactions. Zera Books filters these correctly.
