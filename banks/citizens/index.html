Citizens Bank PDF Statement Format

Citizens Bank personal checking statements list transactions in a simple table: Date, Description, Debits, Credits, Balance. Business account statements use a two-column layout for some page designs — transactions continue in a second column on the same page, which basic extraction tools handle incorrectly (they read rows left to right across columns instead of reading each column top to bottom).

Zera Books reads Citizens Bank's two-column business statement layout correctly, preserving transaction order and amounts.

Former Investors Bank Accounts

Citizens Bank acquired Investors Bank in 2022. Former Investors Bank account holders received rebranded Citizens statements starting in 2023, but some clients may have older Investors Bank PDFs that need converting. These use the Investors Bank layout and are standard PDFs that convert cleanly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download Citizens Bank statements online?

Log into Citizens Bank Online Banking, go to Accounts > Statements to download PDF statements. Transaction activity exports (CSV, OFX, QFX) are available from the Account History page using the Download link.

Can Citizens Bank PDF statements be converted to QuickBooks?

Yes. Citizens Bank PDFs are digital (not scanned), so they convert accurately. Upload the PDF to Zera Books and select QBO or CSV output, then import via QuickBooks Banking > Upload transactions.

Does Citizens Bank CSV export require formatting before QuickBooks import?

Citizens Bank CSV exports include a Balance column and sometimes account summary rows that need to be removed. The date format is MM/DD/YYYY which QuickBooks accepts, but the Balance column will cause a column mapping error. Use Zera Books to handle the cleanup automatically.
