Citizens Bank Statement Formats and What Gets Extracted

Citizens Bank operates across 14 states in the northeastern US, with a strong presence in Rhode Island, Massachusetts, Connecticut, New York, and Pennsylvania. Citizens Bank statements use a clean single-table transaction format for personal accounts, but business checking statements include a distinct section for overdraft protection transfers and sweep entries that are easy to misparse.

Citizens also absorbed Charter One Bank (formerly in the Midwest and Great Lakes) in a rebranding — so some clients may have statements with Charter One branding. Zera Books handles both Citizens Bank and legacy Charter One statement formats without requiring separate configurations.
