RBC Royal Bank Statement Formats and What Gets Extracted

RBC Royal Bank is Canada's largest bank, with the most complex statement formats of the Big Six. Personal RBC statements use a bilingual header block (English and French) that confuses many parsers into treating the French section as transaction data. RBC business banking statements include a "Cheques Paid" section listing cleared cheques in a separate table, distinct from the electronic transaction register.

RBC also issues USD-denominated accounts for business clients with US operations — these produce statements with USD amounts in the same PDF template as CAD accounts, which requires currency-aware extraction to avoid mixing amounts. Zera Books handles all of this correctly: bilingual headers are ignored, cheque sections are merged into the main transaction flow, and each currency is preserved separately in the output.
